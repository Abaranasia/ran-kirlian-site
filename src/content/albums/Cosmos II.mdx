Cosmos Vol. II is the second album on a series of electronic space-ambient dedicated to the magnificence of the Universe and the figure of one of its biggest explorers: Carl Sagan.

This music was written in late nights between 2012 and 2019 in collaboration with Ran Kirlian. All music is based on improvisations on synthesizers. The music on this album is tuned to 432,0 Hz, 432,2 Hz and 440,0 Hz. This is a 24bit release.
